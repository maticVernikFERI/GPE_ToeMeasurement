How to use:
Hold your phone and move it around to see the orientation values change in real-time.
Tap a wheel button to store the current Z-axis (Alpha) value for that wheel.

Axes explanation:
• Alpha: Compass direction (spinning flat)
• Beta: Forward/backward tilt
• Gamma: Left/right tilt
